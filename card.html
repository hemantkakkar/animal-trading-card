Lion

Even though the lion is sometime refrred to as "the king of jungle", it actually only lives on grassland and plains

  • Scientific name: Panthera leo
  • Diet: carnivore
  • Average lifespan: 8-15 years
  • Habitat: scrub,grassland and open woodlands

For all of their roaring,growling and ferociousness, lines are family animal and truly social in thier communities. They usually lives in a group of 15 or more animals called prides. A lion's roar can be heard from as far as 5 miles away.
